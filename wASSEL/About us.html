About us

This non-profitable organization was founded by 3 passionate stdents with the aim to solve the growing problem of waste in our country , Tons of waste every year is produced in our country which kills thousands of animals not only on land but also in the aquatic world , These waste materials disrupt our natural ecosystem. To solve this issue we present you Wassel.

How It Works

Wassel connects your local recyclers and provides a platform where you can sell your waste , Its as simple as a click of a button. When you sell your waste to us we quickly contact other recycling factories and we take you waste to recycle to the nearest factory. If you are lucky you may get a glass bottle made from your wasted glass.
